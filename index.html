Exact Facts

How to Play

This is a game best played with one or more friends. Answer the question with your best guess, then others in the group will guess if the real answer is Over that amount, Under that amount or Exactly right. The game is played until you decide to stop or you run out of friends or patience. Don't like the question, tap Give me a new Fact. Answers are correct as of July 2024.

About

The Threedom crew played a version of this game on the episode I've Done Cameos, Can You Milk Me?. Threedom-listener (Michael Bliss?) suggested it under the name Dumb Over Under who I hope doesn't mind this version created by me. - Jason Shellen
